Project Iqra'

Projek Iqra' is a Malaysia based non-profit organization focusing on education and well-being of less fortunate children and teenagers who are living in community and orphanage. Projek Iqra' is registered under the Registrar of Society on 25th January 2013 (Reg no: PPM-006-14-25012013)


Projek Iqra' was founded by Muhammad Solleh bin Mahfoz, Sharifah Sharina Bt Syed Aswad and Suhaimi Abdul Manan in year 2012. The founders studied together in Universiti Teknologi Malaysia in Kuala Lumpur.


The idea to form Projek Iqra' came up when seeing many children and teenagers in orphanage and community needed help in academic but cannot afford to pay for private tuition. There are also many people who wanted to help but do not know how.
